What's the point of this site? (Roadmap)


This should mainly be for posting gay blogs, showing Geometry Dash stuff I've been working on, publishing mods I've made in one concise place, and organizing art for my oc. Here's a handy dandy roadmap:

v1 requirements:

Links to social media ✔
Self-explanatory

Geometry Dash Media (in progress, due for a revamp similar to the art page)
Links to created levels in both gdbrowser and youtube, as well as links to a personal spreadsheet and my GDDL account for beaten levels. Might include a list of cool levels I like as well.

Publishing Custom Mods ✖
I've made a fair share of mods over the years, including skin mods for stardew valley, lethal company, hollow knight, and a hat in time, a texture pack for minecraft, extra cards in inscryption, a doom wad that replaces doom guy with my oc, etc. This site would hopefully be a nice place to have downloads and links to all of them, with showcases and instructions on how to set them up. (This is mostly for my sake so I don't have to search for random files on my pc lol, but anyone is free to use these or use them as reference for making your own mods)

Gay blog :3 ✖
This would have pictures of the Gay Plush crew I have, as well as other gay stuff, dreams, ramblings, basically a good excuse to have a lot of stuff to write about in a centralized place.
